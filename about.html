About this site

2026 World Cup is an unofficial fan and information page by Studio Nani. It presents the real 2026 World Cup group draw, knockout-stage slot structure, and squad references in a fast, flag-first layout.

The site is designed for quick browsing, bracket planning, and multilingual reference. It is not affiliated with FIFA, national football associations, or any official tournament organizer.

Data approach

Group and knockout information is checked against public tournament schedule references. Squad information is presented as a public-reference MVP and may be updated as official final lists change.
